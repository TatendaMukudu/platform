## 1 · `3cbb46c` — false-success on failed saves

Two writes fell back to an empty object on a failed request, then showed a
success toast and cleared the user's input.

- **Note save** could say "Note saved ✓" on a 401/500, clear the textarea, and
  lose the note. → Now throws on failure, keeps the text, shows the error.
- **Weekly reflection** did the same *and* marked the week complete in local
  storage, locking the user out of retrying. → Now only marks complete on a real
  success.

*File:* `js/member-view.js`. Left the coach quick-check-in's optimistic "saved"
alone (deliberate, low-stakes, pre-existing).

## 2 · `0c01886` — five logic errors ("runs fine, wrong answer")

1. **Memory collapsed distinct life events.** Dedupe merged facts sharing ≥50% of
   words, so "father passed away" and "mother passed away" fused and the second
   was dropped. → Now merges only on ≥70% overlap against the larger phrase.
2. **Activity counted data logged *about* a member as activity *by* them.** A
   coach uploading a sheet made a disengaged athlete look active, suppressing the
   "gone quiet" alert. → Only the member's own inputs (`createdBy === them`) count.
3. **Repetition weight rewarded how much the coach typed** — three coach notes got
   promoted over a real assessment result. → Repetition counts the member's own
   inputs; the strength label reflects the source's true weight.
4. **"What helped similar members" showed 1/1 as evidence.** → Minimum sample of 2.
5. **A cohort of one broke anonymity.** → Minimum cohort of 2 before any
   cohort/shared-pattern framing; low-confidence caveat surfaced in the UI.

*Files:* `server.js`, `ai/privacy.js` (added a conservative SENSITIVE tier so
keyword-free hardship — "I've been struggling, can't cope" — can't slip through as
quotable), `js/app.js` (the caveat).
